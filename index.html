About ACM VJIT

Who We Are

ACM VJIT is the official student chapter of the Association for Computing Machinery at Vidya Jyothi Institute of Technology, Hyderabad. We are a vibrant community of passionate tech enthusiasts, developers, researchers, and future industry leaders.

ACM is the world's largest computing society with over 100,000 members globally. Our chapter bridges the gap between academic learning and real-world technology practice through hands-on workshops, hackathons, guest lectures, and collaborative projects.

🎯 Mission

To advance computing as a science and profession by fostering a culture of innovation, collaboration, and continuous learning among VJIT students.

🔭 Vision

To be the most impactful technical student community in Telangana, producing engineers who lead the next wave of technological transformation.

🚀 Goals

Conduct 12+ technical events annually, mentor 50+ students per semester, and launch 10+ student-led projects that solve real community problems.
